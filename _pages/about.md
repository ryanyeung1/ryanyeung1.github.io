I am a MASc graduate in Biomedical Engineering from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada. I worked in the [Robotics and Control Lab](https://rcl.ece.ubc.ca/home-page/) under the supervision of [Prof. Tim Salcudean](https://ece.ubc.ca/tim-salcudean/).

My [research](/projects/6_humanTeleop/) focused on developing a novel approach to model-mediated teleoperation for tele-robotic ultrasound that incorporated point cloud modelling and stiffness estimation for haptic feedback. My work was part of the [human teleoperation](https://coact.ca/) project which aims to improve access to medical ultrasound in remote communities. I also led the clinical validation of human teleoperation, collaborating with clinicians and remote Indigenous communities.

I previously received a BASc in Biomedical Engineering from the University of British Columbia. During this time, I also worked as a mechatronics engineering co-op at [Aspect Biosystems](https://www.aspectbiosystems.com/) and a test engineering co-op at [Verathon](https://www.verathon.com/).
