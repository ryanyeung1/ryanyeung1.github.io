*Oct 2023 - Dec 2023*

For the final course project of CPSC 540 – Machine Learning at UBC, I collaborated with two other students to evaluate machine learning classification models for medical data prediction. We used scikit-learn to train the classification models on four open-source medical datasets and reported their relative performance.
